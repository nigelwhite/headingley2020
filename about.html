About headingley.org

Many thousands of supportive and positive messages have been exchanged since 2004. Our aim is community self-reliance so asking for, and offering, help is welcome.

Our email networks support relationships in the real world, so residents subscribe with their real names. Messages are not displayed to non-members.

The aim is to increase community resilience. When residents interact and support each other, in daily life, we discover interconnections within our community. This has all sorts of benefits. Please see more on our aims.

Rather than being neutral, we take an ethical position on certain matters. We are in favour of civic space and free sharing. We are against the commodification of public assets for private profit. For further information please see -

  • Policy on commercial use of headingley.org
  • Policy on real names

Our networks are run by residents who volunteer their time.
