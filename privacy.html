Privacy policy

This site keeps your name and email so we can set up your emailing lists.

We ask for information when you register (street, postcode, your view and perspective on community). We use this information to filter out spammers, as well as to know which emailing list to invite you to join.

You can change your emailing list settings on this site. Options for each mailing list are -

unsubscribe / subscribe receive every email or in a bundle (digest) temporarily disable delivery

Emails from residents are sent to subscribers of each list. Once a subscriber you will receive these emails.

Once a subscriber, you can also send emails. You can reply to everyone, or just to the original sender. Your email address is visible to other subscribers, in any email you send.
